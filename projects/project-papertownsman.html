Paper Townsman

Game Development

View on GitHub

Paper Townsman is a pixel-art RPG that blends everyday life with surreal horror. You play as Bettina Jardio, a sharp-witted teen in a crumbling squatter town where reality unravels — streets loop endlessly, people vanish, and "glitches" roam the alleys. When your mother suddenly mentions your long-forgotten father, a search begins that challenges your grip on memory and existence. With a trusty slingshot and limited time, navigate distorted streets, battle anomalies, and choose between unraveling a dark truth or embracing a fragile human connection. Featuring turn-based combat, atmospheric visuals, and multiple endings shaped by your choices, Paper Townsman is a haunting tale of memory, identity, and the things we’re told to forget.
