99: The Card Duel

Game Development, Web Application

View on GitHub

99: The Card Duel is a fast-paced strategy card game where players take turns adding cards to a running total, trying not to be the one to hit or exceed 99. Each round is a tense back-and-forth as you manage your hand and outthink your opponent. Featuring an AI opponent powered by a Minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning, the game challenges players to plan ahead and make calculated moves in this deceptively simple yet deeply strategic duel.
